Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is the perfect summer dessert. The creamy mascarpone custard, delicate ladyfingers, and delicious coffee aroma are an amazing combination.

Ingredients

  • Egg yolks
  • Sugar
  • Heavy cream, cold
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Vanilla
  • Espresso
  • Ladyfingers
  • Bailey's
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Brew the hot coffee and mix with the Bailey's, or a liqueur of your choice.
  2. Make the filling. Place egg yolks and sugar in a bowl and set over pot with simmering water. Whisk constantly until sugar is dissolved and custart thickens. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. Add the mascarpone and vanilla and whisk until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, whip the cold heavy cream to stiff peaks. Fold 1/3 of the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture. Then fold in the remaining whipped cream. Set aside.
  5. Assemble the tiramisu. Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture for 1-2 seconds and place in the bottom of a dish. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat with another layer of ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture.
  6. Refridgerate the tiramisu for at least 6 hours or overnight.
  7. Just before serving, dust with cocoa powder.
